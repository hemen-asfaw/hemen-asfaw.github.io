Hi, my name is Hemen Asfaw, and I’m originally from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I recently completed my B.S. in Statistics and Data Science at Loyola Marymount University and am now pursuing my M.S. in Business Analytics at Cornell University. My passion for working with data began with a simple desire: to answer questions with evidence. Growing up, I saw firsthand the consequences of limited data and the absence of data-driven decision-making, which inspired me to be part of a change that uses data to drive meaningful solutions. During my time at LMU and through my internship at Pfizer, I explored how data can uncover insights, explain outcomes, and even predict future trends. I became especially drawn to bridging the gap between business and data science—translating technical findings into clear, actionable insights for both technical and non-technical audiences—which ultimately led me to pursue my master’s degree.

Through this portfolio, I’m excited to showcase the diverse projects I’ve developed over the past few years through academic coursework, internships, and certification programs. As a recent graduate stepping into a new program, I’m eager to apply my knowledge, contribute to evidence-based decision-making, and continue learning from experts in the field, with the long-term goal of leading initiatives that connect data to real-world impact. Please don’t hesitate to reach out through the contact information provided—I’d be glad to connect!
